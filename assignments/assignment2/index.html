Apple Pie

Did you know that Apple pie is an unofficial symbol or the United States? Apple pie is also known as a signature comfort food. So surprisingly, the main ingredient in apple pie is apple. Who would've known? Apple pie is one of those dishes that can easily be eaten by itself but sometimes tastes better when served with whipped cream or even better, ice cream. Apple pie is the most popular American dish, and is even the state dish in Vermont.

The main ingredients in Apple pie are:

  1. Apples
  2. Flour
  3. Sugar
  4. Milk
  5. Cinnamon
  6. Butter
  7. Salt
