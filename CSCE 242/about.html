THE Hamburger

THE hamburger, also known as just a "burger" is a sandwhich full of grounded mear which is usually beef. The meat is usually fried or grilled and is topped with vegetables, cheese and/or condiments. It has been debated whether the origin of the hambuger is from Germany or America. Either way, it's a very American food and one of the most consumed. Typically, this American burger is served with french fries or chips. Hamburgers/Burgers have become the main item of most fast food chains, besides Chick-fil-A of course. The term Hamburger usually implies a "beef burger", but over the years, many different forms of burgers have come into play. These would be burgers like a turkey burger, a bison burger and veggie burgers.

The main ingredients in a burger are:

  1. Ground meat
  2. Bread
  3. Salt
